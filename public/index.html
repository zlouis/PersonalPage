About

Greetings! My name is Louis and I am a current student at the Rutgers Coding Bootcamp.

I'm a world traveler along with my dslr that I take around with me to photograph the new experiences I encounter along my travels. I'm a dog lover, a dietetic technician registered, a powerlifter a full stack web developer. Currently, I am enrolled in Rutgers Coding Bootcamp. My goal is to not only create practical applications, but build applications that people can truly appreciate. I am comfortable with Javascript, HTML,and CSS and have an extensive use of jQuery. Not only do I enjoy working on the front-end, I also have an understanding of the back-end having worked with Express.js and Sequelize for maintaining web servers. I expect to continue learning new technolgies and tackle any problems or tasks to continue being a well-rounded web developer. Come follow me on my adventure as I make my journey through a career switch into web development!

The specific software development skills in my toolbelt include: JavaScript, jQuery, Node.js, Express, HTML5/CSS3, Bootstrap, Git, Angular.js, Responsive Design, API/JSON, API, Firebase, MySQL, Ajax.
